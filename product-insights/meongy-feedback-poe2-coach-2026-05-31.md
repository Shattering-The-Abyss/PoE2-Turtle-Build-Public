# Product Insight: PoE2 Toon Repair Feedback From Meongy

Date: 2026-05-31

Source context: live PoE2 build-repair workflow for Meongy's Spirit Walker / companion direction.

## Feedback

> I use it and its good though
> Straight concise to the point and minus snarky useless remarks

## Why This Matters

This is strong product feedback because it describes the user-facing value in plain terms:

- The output is useful enough to use in real gameplay.
- The advice is concise instead of over-explaining.
- The recommendation is direct enough to act on.
- The tone avoids snark, padding, and generic filler.

For PoE2 toon repair, this matters more than producing a perfect theoretical build. Players usually arrive with a fuzzy problem: their damage feels wrong, they keep dying, the guide they copied does not match their current character, or they do not know which gear slot is causing the failure.

The product value is not "AI makes the game idle." The value is coaching the player through the exact current character state so they understand what to fix next.

## Capability Signal

This feedback validates the current operating posture:

- Read the current character state.
- Identify the practical bottleneck.
- Separate gear, passive tree, gem setup, and execution problems.
- Give the next few actions rather than a full abstract lecture.
- Keep the human player in charge of decisions.

## Product Lesson

For public PoE2 repair reports, optimize for:

- concise verdict first
- concrete fixes by priority
- explain only the parts needed for player understanding
- no performative sarcasm
- no generic "follow a guide" answer
- no pretending to know exact hidden monster math when item and build direction are enough

This is a useful positioning line:

> Not an idle-game autopilot. A concise build repair coach for the character you actually have.
