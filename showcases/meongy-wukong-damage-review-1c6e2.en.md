# Meongy Wukong Damage Review - 1c6e2

Source: `https://poe.ninja/poe2/pob/1c6e2`

Player note: ignore Mageblood because it was borrowed. The goal is simple: push higher Wukong damage.

## Current Read

This is not a broken character. The build is already on the right Spirit Walker companion lane.

The important detail is that the Wukong damage engine is `Companion: Mighty Silverfist`, which is effectively the Diretusk companion with four support slots. That means the upgrade plan should not chase spear damage, Whirling Slash damage, or generic player attack damage first. The build should feed the companion engine.

Current visible foundation:

- Level 79 Spirit Walker Huntress
- Life around 1624, Energy Shield around 351, Spirit around 232
- Fire and cold resistance are fine, lightning is lower, chaos is usable
- Several good minion/companion idols are already equipped
- Sceptre, amulet, and helmet already carry the right `+ to Minion Skills` direction
- Mageblood is borrowed, so the permanent version still needs real belt/ring/shield resistance coverage

## Main Diagnosis

The build has the correct idea, but the next upgrades need to stay disciplined:

1. Treat Mighty Silverfist as the main carry.
2. Prioritize minion/companion gem levels before small player damage gains.
3. Improve companion damage and attack speed on idols and gear.
4. Keep permanent defenses honest without counting borrowed Mageblood.
5. Do not spend first on spear hit damage unless it also helps the companion plan.

The current support direction on the 4-link Mighty Silverfist is already much more important than the player spear package. If a future swap is tested, compare direct companion attack scaling first before committing harder to bleed/fire-only scaling.

## Upgrade Priority

### 1. Better Sceptre

The current sceptre is already structurally correct: Spirit, `+2 to all Minion Skills`, ally damage, and companion damage rune/value.

Only replace it if the new sceptre keeps the core package:

- `+2 to Level of all Minion Skills`
- high Spirit
- allies in presence damage or companion damage
- no major loss of reservation/spirit comfort

Search: [sceptre filter](https://www.pathofexile.com/trade2/search/poe2/Runes%20of%20Aldur/X3bzRkRJuP)

### 2. Better Helmet

The helmet should keep `+2 to all Minion Skills`. Upgrade only if the new helmet adds actual defensive value.

Look for:

- `+2 to Level of all Minion Skills`
- life or useful resistance
- real armour/evasion/energy shield base

Search: [helmet filter](https://www.pathofexile.com/trade2/search/poe2/Runes%20of%20Aldur/V5QzLynlfp)

### 3. Better Idols

The idols are already good enough to prove the direction. The next target is higher density per idol slot.

Look for:

- minions deal increased damage
- minion attack/cast speed
- companion damage or companion attack speed if available
- minion elemental resistance only if the companion is dying

Search: [idol filter](https://www.pathofexile.com/trade2/search/poe2/Runes%20of%20Aldur/OgdzbpWruE)

### 4. Permanent Ring Coverage

Because Mageblood is borrowed, do not trust the current defensive picture too much. Before harder content, use rings/shield/belt to make the character stable without borrowed power.

Look for:

- life
- lightning resistance
- total elemental resistance
- attributes only if needed

Search: [ring filter](https://www.pathofexile.com/trade2/search/poe2/Runes%20of%20Aldur/QLbz2rRmfw)

## What Not To Do

- Do not rebuild around player spear damage.
- Do not overpay for a weapon that loses Spirit or minion levels.
- Do not count Mageblood as part of the real build.
- Do not buy random DPS-looking items unless they improve Mighty Silverfist or companion uptime.

## Short Verdict

The car is pointed in the right direction. To push Wukong damage, keep feeding Mighty Silverfist: gem levels, companion damage, companion speed, and enough permanent defense to keep the pilot alive.
