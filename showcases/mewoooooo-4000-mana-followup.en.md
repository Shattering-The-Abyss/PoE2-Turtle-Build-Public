## Short Answer

Raising this character from `3066` mana to `4000` mana is not a one-item tweak. It is roughly a `+934 displayed mana` project.

The realistic path is:

1. First push to about `3600-3700` mana while also improving life, chaos resistance, and physical hit comfort.
2. Then finish the last `300-400` mana through better jewels/passives, a premium shield/helmet, or a very strong amulet upgrade.

If you chase only the mana number, the character can become worse: higher mana on paper, but still fragile in real fights.

## Recommended Path

### Phase 1: Make the build better, not just bigger

Target:

```text
3600-3700 mana
more life
better chaos resistance
better physical hit comfort
keep Spirit and spell level intact
```

Buy or craft in this order:

1. Belt with life + mana + chaos or resistance.
2. Shield with mana or high ES, plus life/resistance if possible.
3. Rare gloves with life + mana + resistance, then test against `Doedre's Tenure`.
4. Boots with movement speed + mana + life/resistance.
5. Upgrade the weaker ring if the price is reasonable.

### Phase 2: Finish the 4000 mana push

Only after Phase 1 feels stable:

1. Look for premium shield/helmet upgrades.
2. Check whether passive/jewel changes can add the last `250-350` mana.
3. Only replace the amulet if the new one keeps the important jobs: Spirit, spell level, and mana.

## What Not To Do

Do not blindly replace the amulet. It is carrying mana, Spirit, and spell level.

Do not replace every defensive slot with pure mana gear. This build is already asking mana to do too much work.

Do not assume `4000 mana` automatically means the character is repaired. The real target is:

```text
high mana + enough life + enough chaos resistance + enough physical comfort
```

## Manual Trade Filter Recipes

These are not exact marketplace links. They are the filter logic to use when shopping.

### Belt

Required:

- `+# to maximum Mana`
- `+# to maximum Life`

Add one or more:

- `+#% to Chaos Resistance`
- `+#% to Fire/Cold/Lightning Resistance`
- useful attributes

### Shield

Required:

- high Energy Shield or `+# to maximum Mana`
- useful resistance

Strong adds:

- maximum Life
- all elemental resistance
- chaos resistance
- block or armour/ES hybrid value

### Gloves

Required:

- maximum Life
- maximum Mana
- resistance

Optional:

- cast speed
- spell damage
- Intelligence
- useful defensive suffix

This slot must be tested against `Doedre's Tenure`, because losing spell damage may reduce burst.

### Ring

Required:

- high maximum Mana
- resistance or chaos resistance

Strong adds:

- `% increased maximum Mana`
- life
- Intelligence
- mana regeneration

### Helmet

Required:

- high maximum Mana or strong Energy Shield
- resistance

Strong adds:

- life
- Intelligence
- chaos resistance

## Final Verdict

`4000 mana` is achievable, but it is a small rebuild, not a small adjustment.

Best practical plan:

```text
3066 mana
-> repair belt / shield / gloves / boots / weak ring
-> reach roughly 3600-3700 mana with better defenses
-> finish 4000 through jewels/passives/premium shield or helmet
```

The clean version of the advice:

> Do not buy the number. Buy the operating floor. If the upgrades make mana bigger and the character harder to kill, they are correct. If they only make the mana number bigger, they are bait.
